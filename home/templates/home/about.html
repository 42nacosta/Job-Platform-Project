About This Project

This web app is a CS2340 (Objects & Design) group project built by Nathaniel Acosta, Jackson O'Connell, Adem Poshka, and Maitreya Kayyar.. It follows the Django MVT (Model–View–Template) architecture and targets a lightweight job-seeking and matching workflow for early-career candidates and recruiters.

What You Can Do
  • Browse and search job postings from the home page.
  • Open a job detail page to view the full description.
  • Sign up / log in and manage your account (in progress).
  • Control what recruiters can see via Privacy Settings.
In Scope for This Class
  • Core job CRUD (post, edit, delete) with ownership checks.
  • Recruiter vs. Job-Seeker roles (basic authorization).
  • Applicant privacy controls (field-level visibility).
  • Clean, testable views and templates (no magic).
Architecture at a Glance (MVT)
  • Models: Job, Profile (1:1 with User for roles & privacy).
  • Views: Readable functions handling queries, auth gates, and context.
  • Templates: Bootstrap-styled pages extending a shared base.html.
Roadmap (Next Iterations)
  • Job applications with status tracking.
  • Saved jobs & simple recommendations.
  • Recruiter pipeline basics (shortlist, notes).
  • Map views for job locations and commute radius.
